I have seen the organization go through many changes and been part of implementing those changes, we went from doing everything on paper and carrying around binders full of documents to implementing new modern processes into all of our functions, we have implemented new apps, computer systems and just recently upgraded to salesforce. I have been a champion of change and supported my team through changes. I have worked with cross-functional teams to improve processes. I have collaberated with other departments to streamline operations and improve collection outcomes. I have been able to lead projects including moving our entire blood collection center from the old location in Orem to the new modern location in Lehi. I have been able to lead my team to meet and exceed goals while constantly growing and adapting to the ever changing blood collection business.
